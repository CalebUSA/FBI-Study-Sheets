Dear Friend,

Thank you for taking the FBI Practice Test test. I had a message I wanted to give you.


    Someday you and I must stand before God. We cannot escape it. God warned about this in His Word, the Holy Bible. (Hebrews 9:27 "And as it is appointed unto men once to die, but after this the judgment.") This day will be very horrifying for those who have rejected the Lord Jesus Christ during their brief time on earth. (Hebrews 2:3 "How shall we escape, if we neglect so great salvation; which at the first began to be spoken by the Lord, and was confirmed unto us by them that heard him?")


Please read the following message to see how you can be right with God.

All have sinned!

You do not go to the doctor unless you see you are sick and you do not call upon the Saviour unless you see you are a sinner
Let's take a brief test to see if you are a sinner.
  1. Have you ever stolen anything? (Even as small as a pen or a thumb drive)
  2. If you were caught, did you lie about it? (If you were not, if you were caught do you think you would have told the truth even if it sent you to prison?)
  3. Have you ever looked at anything that someone else had and wanted it? (Or may be something similar to what someone else had?)
  4. Did you lie about any of the above questions?
If you answered yes to any of the above questions, you have broken one of the Ten Commandments and we have only gone over 3 of the ten. This is what the Bible has to say about this: James 2:10 (For whosoever shall keep the whole law, and yet offend in one point, he is guilty of all.) Romans 3:10 (There is none righteous, no, not one) Romans 3:23, (For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God)

The penalty for sin is death

The Bible says in Romans 6:23a "For the wages of sin is death" If you sin you will die. You cannot enter Heaven as a sinner. (1 Corinthians 6:9; "Know ye not that the unrighteous shall not inherit the kingdom of God") The Bible tells us that there is a penalty for sin...It is eternal (forever) death and separation from God. If you continue in your sin, your name will not be found in the Book of Life nor will you go to Heaven. In Revelation 20 it says "And whosoever was not found written in the book of life was cast into the lake of fire. This is the second death."

Good works CAN'T save you

Romans 10:3 "For they, being ignorant of God's righteousness, and going about to establish their own righteousness, have not submitted themselves unto the righteousness of God." Church membership, baptism, praying a prayer, giving to the poor, living a moral life, or doing any other good deeds or rituals cannot make you righteous before God

The need: A Saviour to save you

2 Corinthians 5:21 "For he [God] hath made him [Jesus] to be sin for us, who knew no sin; that we might be made the righteousness of God in him." 1 Peter 3:18 "For Christ also hath once suffered for sins, the just for the unjust, that he might bring us to God, being put to death in the flesh, but quickened by the Spirit." How much God loves us as it says in John 3:16 "For God so loved the world [you] that he gave his only begotten Son [Jesus], that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life." He allowed His own Son to be crucified on a cruel cross so we could be saved

You can have this righteousness right now

Romans 10:4, 9, 13 "For Christ is the end of the law for righteousness to everyone that believeth. That if thou shalt confess with thy mouth the Lord Jesus, and shalt believe in thine heart [NOT JUST PRAYING A PRAYER] that God hath raised him from the dead, thou shalt be saved. For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved." Your eternal soul is one thing you must not lose. Don't spend another second away from the Lord Jesus Christ. You are not promised another second. The Bible says "Behold, now is the accepted time; behold, now is the day of salvation." (2 Corinthians 6:2) He knocks at your door. Won't you let him in? This may be the last call. Notice again His wonderful promise "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved."
At this point you have a choice; you can either deny or accept Christ. That is the only two choices. You cannot wait a while for salvation. Every second that you are living you are making that choice. What will your answer be this second? Remember Hebrews 3:15b "...Today if ye will hear his voice, harden not your hearts."

AGAIN, YOU HAVE TWO CHOICES. WHAT IS YOUR CHOICE?

ACCEPT JESUS CHRIST'S PAYMENT ALONE FOR YOUR SIN



OR

DENY JESUS CHRIST'S PAYMENT ON THE CROSS AND DO NOT ALLOW JESUS TO BE MY PERSONAL LORD AND ONLY SAVIOUR



If you chose that last choice, you are choosing out of your own free will to spend an eternity in Hell.

If you have prayed and believed in your heart that Christ saved you because of what he did for you, you are now a Christian. Nothing can take that away from you. You cannot lose your salvation (John 10:27-29 "And I give unto them eternal life; and they shall never perish, neither shall any man pluck them out of my hand. My Father, which gave them me, is greater than all; and no man is able to pluck them out of my Father's hand." Hebrews 13:5c "I will never leave thee, nor forsake thee." Deuteronomy 4:31 "(For the LORD thy God is a merciful God;) He will not forsake thee, neither destroy thee, nor forget the covenant... which he swear)" Praise Him! I have been praying for you as I wrote this. Next you need to find a Church where God's word is preached and Truth is taught. If you would like I will suggest a local church for you. I pray you will grow in your new faith as you read and meditate on His Word. Welcome to the family of God. Now here is that local church I mentioned.
